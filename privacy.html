Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Fresh by Mart ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you visit freshbymart.com.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website we automatically collect basic analytics data such as pages viewed, clicks, device type, browser, and general location. This helps us improve our site. When you book a cleaning, your booking and payment information is collected and processed through our third party payment processor. We do not store your payment card details on our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to schedule and provide our bin cleaning services, communicate with you about your service, improve our website, and respond to your questions.

Third Party Services

We use a third party payment processor to handle bookings and payments. Their handling of your information is governed by their own privacy policy. We use website hosting and analytics tools that may collect anonymous usage data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information with the service providers we use to operate our business.

Your Rights

You may request to see, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Contact us at info@freshbymart.com

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience and gather analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to info@freshbymart.com
